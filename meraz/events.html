      Stage Presence: Did the band use the stage effectively? Did the band appear positive about their performance? Did the band interact effectively on stage? How did the band cope with any problems they may have encountered?

      Conformance to contest rules: Did the band followed the rules stated in the contest i.e. time limit, profanity?

Tech Riddles

  • Description
    The world’s greatest invention is hidden somewhere to protect it from getting into wrong hands. Numerous hint and traps have been laid to protect it. Only true geniuses would be able to find it. You and your three friends will have to decode hints, solve various puzzles and deactivate traps to find the Treasure. Are you up for the challenge?

  • Event Outline

    It is a group event where a group of 4 people will participate. The main goal of the event is to find the treasure (invention). The event will start with a small quiz to test their abilities (and mainly to provide a time gap between two teams). The quiz will contain logical and aptitude questions. Once a team clears the quiz, they will be given a hint, which they have to decode in order to get the location of the next stage. It will be a small region with puzzles hidden. These puzzles could be found by just looking around. The team has to solve these puzzles to obtain different keys. These keys will fit into another puzzle which will give a hint for the final stage location. The final stage will be the inventor’s lab. To enter the lab, teams have to deactivate different alarms and traps by solving some electrical circuits and hacking the system (which they can do with the help of the hint in the previous stage). Once they enter the lab the just have to find the invention and the win the game.

  • Event description
    Theme

    The event is based on the story of an inventor who made the greatest invention of all time. But before presenting it to the public he was being threatened by some people to hand over his invention. But he already knew something like this was going to happen and was already prepared for this. To protect it from getting it into the wrong hands, he hid his invention and set hints and traps so that someday it will be found.

    Main goal

    The main goal of the event is to find the invention by solving hints and puzzles before the other teams.

    First stage

    The game will start with a quiz of 10 to 15 questions. The quiz will contain logical and aptitude questions. The questions will be of objective or fill in the blanks type which will be solved in the question paper itself. As the team clears the quiz the will be given a hint card. The hint card will be used in the next stage. After three teams cleared the quiz there will be a time penalty of 5-10 minutes for other teams (to create a time gap between teams).

    Second stage

    The second stage will be a small region (like the lobby of the academic block), where puzzles will be camouflaged and the teams have to find them by looking around. The hint card from the previous stage will contain a hint for the location of next stage and hint for a pattern for a combinational lock. There will be 4 puzzles. Three will have the keys to solve the fourth puzzle and the fourth will contain the hint for the next stage.

    Third stage

    The third stage will be the old lab of the scientist where the invention is hidden. There will be alarm security in the lab and players will have to deactivate the alarm and open the lock to enter the lab. In there the will have to find the invention hidden somewhere. The game will be over when a team find the invention.

Real Steel

Real Steel

    Real Steel is a Robotics Competition, where the challenge is to build a manually controlled,Real Robo Monster, which will go through two separate stages Robo-Soccer and Robo-Sumo.

  • Description
    1. Robo-Soccer(Stage 1)

    Robo-soccer, a game in which your robot has to push the football into the goal and simultaneously stop the opponents’ robot from scoring. The team with more goals at the end wins the match. The competitor will be decided on the spot.

    2. Robo-Sumo(Stage 2)

    Robo-sumo is a sport in which two robots attempt to push each other out of a circle. The engineering challenges are for the robot to find its opponent and to push it out of the flat circular arena. A robot should also avoid leaving the arena, usually by means of a sensor that detects the edge. In Robo-sumo, you can either win by pushing your opponent out or if they exit the arena due to their own excess momentum, or by disabling the opponent by colliding with it. The competitor will be decided on the spot.

  • ROBOT Specifications

Ro-Boat

Ro-Boat

    Ro-Boat is a Technical Challenge, where a wirelessly controlled boat is to be designed using laws of physics and technical skills. The excellence and accuracy of that boat will be challenged through two different stages "The Time Trial" and "Fill The Pocket".

  • Description

    The game will be in two stages

    1. Time-Trial

    The competition starts with this stage. This stage is SPEED-TIME round. The participating teams will have to bring their boat into the specially designed Racing Arena and complete the race within a timezone. (Note: The same bot has to be used throughout the event, hence no modifications shall be made in the final made boat throughout the day. Only modifications allowed - renewing power supply, repairing in case of boat damage during the competition and activation/deactivation of sensors for automation). The race will be like "race against time" , as the boat completes the race , the completion time will be noted and the points will be given as follows.

    • Gold: if the team finishes the race within the golden time range then they'll be given 5 points.
    • Silver: if the team finishes the race within the silver time range then they'll be given 3 points.
    • Bronze: if the team finishes the race within the bronze time range then they'll be given 2 points.
    • If a team fails to complete the race within any of the time range mentioned above then they'll be given a zero.

    2. Fill The Pockets

    This is the second stage of the "Ro-Boat" competition. The participating teams will have to bring their boats into the specially designed Racing Arena and compete 2 at a time for a specific time zone.The arena consists of slots of different colors and the boat has to fill the slots with a ball. Point distribution:

    • If participant fill the slot with the ball of different color then he'll be given 1 point.
    • If participant fill the slot with the ball of same color then he'll be given 2 points.
    • If the participant file slot full of hurdles i.e. moving slots etc. then he'll be given 3 points.
    • If the participant fill the occupied slot with another different colored ball then he'll be given 0.5 points.
    • If the participant fill the occupied slot with another same colored ball then he'll be given 1 point.
    • The team with highest number of points i.e the stage 1 + stage 2, will be the winner.


  • Rules

    The teams need not necessarily participate in all the stages of the competition. They can participate in both or in some specific stage as per their choice. However, zero (0) points would be given to the team for a stage they don’t participate in and cumulative score would be calculated considering all the stages, even if they don’t participate in all.
